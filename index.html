Call for papers

Smart cities result from the increasingly urgent need to orient our lives towards sustainability. Therefore, these cities use infrastructure, innovation, and technology to improve the quality of life of their citizens.

Being a strategic issue that brings new challenges, the organizing committee invites the academic community to participate in the V Iberoamerican Congress of Smart Cities (ICSC-CITIES 2023). The congress will be a discussion forum to create synergies among diﬀerent research groups to favor the development of Smart Cities. Therefore, authors are invited to register and submit manuscripts and contribute to knowledge development and integration in diﬀerent scenarios.

ICSC-CITIES 2023 will take place on November 13-17, 2023, in Mexico City and Cuernavaca. From 13 to 15, conferences will occur at Centro de Investigación en Computación del Instituto Politécnico Nacional in Mexico City, and from 16 to 17, conferences will occur at Universidad Autónoma del Estado de Morelos, in Cuernavaca, Morelos, Mexico.
